because the resize handler didn't refit canvases robustly.
  → Two-layer fix: (1) the resize handler now schedules redraw via

    reads bounding rects, with a debounce flag to coalesce same-tick
    bursts. (2) Added a matchMedia `(resolution: <DPR>dppx)` listener that

    with the new resolution on each fire so subsequent zooms still
    catch.
